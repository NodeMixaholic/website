Step 1: Download the Windows 11 ISO file.

Step 2: Create a bootable USB drive or DVD. To do this, you will need a USB drive with at least 8GB of storage space and a DVD burner.

Step 3: Insert the USB drive or DVD into your computer.

Step 4: Reboot your computer and enter the BIOS setup. To do this, you will need to press a specific key during the boot process. This key is usually F2, F10, or Del.

Step 5: Once in the BIOS setup, you will need to change the boot order so that the USB drive or DVD is the first device to boot from.

Step 6: Save the changes and exit the BIOS setup.

Step 7: The Windows 11 installation process will now begin. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

Step 8: Once the installation is complete, you will need to activate Windows 11. To do this, you will need to enter your product key.

Step 9: After activation, you will need to install any necessary drivers and updates.

Step 10: Finally, you can begin using Windows 11. Enjoy!
